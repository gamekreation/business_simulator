# Game Database - Buildings, Recipes, Skills & Resource Nodes

This document provides a complete registry of the economic entities, manufacturing recipes, skills, and resource systems currently implemented in the simulator.

---

## 1. Natural Resource Nodes & Extractors

### Natural Deposits
* **🌾 Fertile Land**: Extracted by `agricultural_farm` to harvest **Crops (`fertile_land_crop`)** or **Cotton**.
* **🌲 Forest**: Extracted by `lumber_mill` to harvest **Wood**.
* **🪨 Stone Deposit**: Extracted by `stone_quarry` to harvest **Stone**.
* **⛏ Iron Deposit**: Extracted by `iron_mine` to harvest **Iron Ore**.
* **⚫ Coal Deposit** *(Industrial Age)*: Extracted by `coal_mine` to harvest **Coal**.
* **🛢 Oil Field** *(Industrial Age)*: Extracted by `oil_rig` to harvest **Crude Oil**.
* **⛰ Limestone Deposit** *(Industrial Age)*: Extracted by `limestone_quarry` to harvest **Limestone**.
* **🟠 Copper Deposit** *(Tech Age)*: Extracted by `copper_mine` to harvest **Copper Ore**.
* **💎 Silicon Deposit** *(Tech Age)*: Extracted by `silicon_mine` to harvest **Silicon**.
* **☢ Uranium Deposit** *(Nuclear Age)*: Extracted by `uranium_mine` to harvest **Uranium Ore**.

## 2. Infrastructure & Auxiliary Buildings

* **🏛 Administrative Town Hall (`town_hall`)**: National HQ. Upgrades unlock technological Ages.
* **🗺 Regional HQ Hub (`regional_hq`)**: Auto-placed in unlocked regions. Coordinates local builder queues, logistics, and imports.
* **🚚 Logistics HQ Operations (`logistics_hq`)**: Fleet Operations. Unlocks cargo vehicle purchasing.
* **👷 Builder Company HQ (`builder_company`)**: Speeds up regional construction slots.
* **🚢 International Trade Center (`trade_center`)**: Unlocks raw materials imports directly to the local warehouse.
* **📦 Warehouse (`warehouse`)**: Increases local region warehouse storage capacity (VU limits).

## 4. Retail & Services Portfolio

### Retail Stores (Consumes Finished Products ➔ Yields Cash)
* **🍔 Food Shop**: Sells **Food** to local customers.
* **🛒 Grocery Shop**: Sells **Grocery Goods** to local customers.
* **👕 Clothing Shop**: Sells **Clothes** to local customers.
* **🪑 Furniture Shop**: Sells **Furniture** to local customers.
* **💊 Medical Shop**: Sells **Medicine** to local customers.
* **📱 Electronics Shop**: Sells **Electronics** to local customers.
* **⛽ Gas Station**: Sells **Fuel** to local customers.

### Service Businesses (Consumes Goods or operates on service contracts)
* **🏠 Interior Design Studio**: Consumes **Furniture** to deliver interior services.
* **Auto Service Garage (`garage`)**: Consumes **Car Parts** to repair customer vehicles.
* **📐 Architecture Firm**: Delivers architectural layout services.
* **💼 Consulting Firm**: Delivers Corporate strategies and optimization services.
* **🏨 Luxury Hotel Resort**: Delivers high-end tourism and hospitality services.

## 5. Executive Skill Trees

Players invest points earned from corporate operations to acquire global bonuses:

### 🟢 Production Branch
* **Extraction Technology**: Increases global resource extraction rates.
* **Manufacturing Efficiency**: Boosts factory processing speeds.
* **Supply Logistics**: Reduces transit truck delivery delays.

### 🟡 Marketing Branch
* **Branding Strategy**: Increases selling prices across all retail shops.
* **Customer Acquisition**: Boosts visitor count and sales volume.
* **Territory Expansion**: Discounts regional grid unlock fees.

### 🔵 Finance Branch
* **Maintenance Optimization**: Lowers building salaries and daily upkeep fees.
* **Corporate Taxation**: Minimizes monthly tax penalties.
* **Capital Liquidation**: Increases refunds earned when selling assets.
