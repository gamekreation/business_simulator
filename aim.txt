# Project Specification - Business Empire Simulator (V0.8+)

## Objective

Build a premium, single-player RTS-style Business Empire and Logistics simulator to validate the core economic gameplay loop of extracting, refining, shipping, and retailing goods across a multi-region landscape.

This prototype serves as a foundation for testing complex supply chain optimizations, regional logistics flow, and technological age progressions.

---

# Technology Stack

* **Frontend**: Next.js 16 (App Router), React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, Lucide Icons, Mermaid Diagrams.
* **Backend**: Supabase Database API (PostgreSQL), Client Save sanitization.
* **UX/UI Layout**: Fully responsive CSS Grid and Flex layouts optimized for Mobile Landscape orientation (supporting rotation prompts and auto-scaling components).

---

# Primary Core Gameplay Loop

```mermaid
graph TD
    Age[Administrative HQ Upgrade] --> |Unlocks Resource Nodes| Node[Raw Resource Extraction]
    Node --> |Auto-Haul to Silo| WH[Regional Warehouse]
    WH --> |Supply Inputs| Fact[Manufacturing Factory]
    Fact --> |Refine to Products| Retail[Retail Shops & Showrooms]
    Retail --> |Sell to Customers| Money[Earn Corporate Revenue]
    Money --> |Reinvest| Upgrade[Upgrade Buildings & Logistics Fleet]
    Upgrade --> |Consolidate| Merge[Merge into Corporate HQs]
    Merge --> |Expand Nationally| Age
```

1. **Extract**: Harvest raw materials (Stone, Wood, Iron, Crops) from deposit nodes using specialized extractors.
2. **Deliver (Automated)**: Local silos automatically queue shipments to transfer goods to regional warehouses.
3. **Manufacture**: Feed raw materials to factories to craft processed goods (Food, Clothes, Furniture, Steel, Mortar, etc.).
4. **Distribute**: Fleet vehicles haul manufactured goods to retail shops.
5. **Sell**: Retail stores sell goods based on customer demand, converting resources into cash flow.
6. **Upgrade & Merge**: Reinvest profits to level up operations, purchase advanced heavy haulers, and merge small businesses into large-scale corporate entities.
7. **Expand Regions**: Level up the Administrative HQ to progress through technological ages, unlocking new permanent regions.

# Architecture & Systems

### 1. Dynamic Grid Map
* **Six Permanent Regions**: 
  * Agriculture ($14\times14$)
  * Forestry ($15\times15$)
  * Industrial ($20\times20$)
  * Technology ($16\times16$)
  * Healthcare ($15\times15$)
  * Commercial ($16\times16$)
* Each region runs grid layouts containing resource nodes, Transit Roads, local factories, warehouses, and shops. Only elements in the active region render.

### 2. Isolated Regional Inventories
* Resources, storage volume thresholds (`maxStorageVolume`), and fleet assignments are partitioned per-region. Switching regions loads that region's local inventories.

### 3. Road Pathfinder & Connectivity Scoping
* All building structures must connect via roads to a local region hub (such as the `regional_hq` or `logistics_hq`). Adjacency and pathfinders traverse roads scoped to the active region.

### 4. Logistics Fleet & Dedicated Routing
* **Autonomous Hauling**: Trucks automatically dispatch and transport resources based on active shipments.
* **Dedicated Route Assignments**: Trucks can be locked to general tasks or dedicated resource routes (e.g. Crops, Wood, Iron Ore).
* **Liquidations**: Players can sell vehicles to recover 70% of the original cost and upgrade level expenses.

### 5. Administrative HQ & Technological Ages
The **Administrative HQ** level governs national progression and unlocks era-appropriate mineral extraction:
* **Foundation Age** (Stone, Wood, Iron, Crops) - Lvl 1-3.
* **Industrial Age** (Unlocks Coal, Oil, Limestone) - Lvl 4-6.
* **Technology Age** (Unlocks Copper, Silicon) - Lvl 7-9.
* **Nuclear Age** (Unlocks Uranium) - Lvl 10+.

### 6. Corporate Merges
Consolidate smaller chains into massive HQs (e.g. Automobile Company, Furniture Brand) when layout and asset conditions are satisfied. Merges inherit building level quality ratings.

### 7. Time Control & Scalable Presets
* **Adjustable Simulation Speed**: Time presets dynamically scale construction and transport tick durations:
  * *Debug*: 10 seconds per game day.
  * *Fast*: 30 seconds per game day.
  * *Dev*: 60 seconds per game day (default).
  * *Beta*: 120 seconds per game day.
  * *Release*: 320 seconds per game day.

### 8. Financial Operations (Salaries, Debt & Taxes)
* **Monthly Salaries**: Flat payroll fees deducted at the end of every game month (₹50 per constructed building).
* **Accounts Payable Debt**: If cash flows are negative, unpaid payroll is consolidated into deferred liabilities with a 10-day repayment countdown. If unpaid, a 5% interest penalty is applied.
* **National Corporate Taxes**: 4% corporate tax is deducted monthly from players holding cash reserves exceeding ₹50,000.

### 9. Durability & Wear-and-Tear Decay
* **Integrity Degradation**: Buildings lose durability naturally over time (Extractors decay in 90 game days, factories in 360 days).
* **Automatic Safeguards**: Extractors pause production and freeze integrity loss when their silos fill up. Factories pause and freeze decay when hitting target quotas.
* **Maintenance & Repair**: Leveling up maintenance departments reduces degradation rates.

### 10. Tutorial Onboarding On-Rails
* **Advisor System**: 11-step interactive onboarding program guiding players from initial road placement through extractor setups, refinery operations, warehousing, logistics purchases, and retail selling.

### 11. Achievements & Premium Currency
* **Gems Achievements**: 10 distinct achievement branches (e.g. capital milestones, fleet size, merged corporations) rewarding players with standard currency and premium Gems.
